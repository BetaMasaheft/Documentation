    Description of content

    Item ms_i1

    Fols. 1r-4r Titles of the Canons of the Council of Nicaea 1, 2. [Complete]

    Language of text: Latin

    Item ms_i1.1

    Fol. 1r **No record for Work LIT3155NicaeaIntrLa** = Introduction to the titles of the Canons of the Council of Nicaea 1, 2. ** [Complete]

    Incipit: Nicena prima Synodus Integra lxxxiiij canones continens, aethiopica lingua conscripta Argume(n)tu(m) cu(m) canonu(m) xx quos aethiopes etia(m), sicut graeci, ac Latini habent, tu(m) etiam lxxxxiiij, quos ipsi praecepta uocant, statim inuentes.

    Language of text: Latin

    Item ms_i1.2

    Fol. 1v **No record for Work LIT3152Nicaea1tLa** = Titles of the Canons of the Council of Nicaea 1. ** [Complete]

    Incipit: Argumenta. xx. canonum qui cu(m) apud graecos, tum etiam apud latinos exta(n)t. De eunuchis, et qui se ipsos absciderunt, ac circu(m)ciseru(n)t.

    Explicit: De flectendo genua.

    Language of text: Latin

    Item ms_i1.3

    Fols. 2r-3v **No record for Work LIT3153Nicaea2tLa** = Titles of the Canons of the Council of Nicaea 2. ** [Complete]

    Incipit: Argumenta lxxxxiiij canonu(m), quos ipsi aethiopes, praecepta uocant, qui primum Niceae, ut testat(ur) D. Athanasius, aediti sunt. ex quibus xx. canones, quos nos habemus, tra(n)scripti su(n)t. De lunaticis non ordinandis (...)

    Explicit: De assistendo infirmis et pauperibus 84. Haec Argumenta invenies infra post xx. primos canones diuulgatos.

    Language of text: Latin

    Item ms_i1.4

    Fol. 4r **No record for Work LIT3154Nicaea37La** = Canon 37 of the Council of Nicaea 2. ** [Complete]

    Incipit: Canones quidam latine co(n)uersi Canon xxxvij. De Numero Patriarchorum. Sint Patriarchae in orbe quatuor (...)

    Explicit: Qui aute(m) prevaricatus fuerit hoc praeceptu(m), quod nos, sanctoru(m) congregatio, constituimus. Anathema.

    Language of text: Latin
