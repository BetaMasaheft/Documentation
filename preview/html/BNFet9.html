    Item ms_i1

    ff. 1-97r Psalter Dawit [Complete]

    Language of text: Gǝʿǝz

    Rubric: The ecclesiastic division of the psalms is noted in the margins.

    The number of the verses (ቃል፡), which is indicated at the end of each psalm, isn't always exact.

    The text of this version has been edited in Potken 1513 and Ludolf 1701. This manuscript shows slight differences in the arguments.

    Nearly all salāms end with invocations for a certain **No record for Person PRS10805estifano** = ʾƎsṭifānos **, for whom the manuscript has certainly been produced.
