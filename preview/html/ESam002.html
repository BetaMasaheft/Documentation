Origin

Original Location: Asir Mätira / AM

Date: 1450-1550

Second half of the 15th - mid 16th century (?). Additio 7 refers to the role of King Zärʾa Yaʿqob (r. 1434-68) and Bishop (here called ṗaṗas) Yoḥannǝs in the foundation of the monastery, but it is written in a later hand. The latter arrived to Ethiopia in 1438-39 (bearing the title ʾeṗisqoṗos), together with metropolitans Mikāʾel and Gabrǝʾel (after 1458, there was no bishop in Ethiopia). Cf. S.C. MUNRO-HAY, Ethiopia and Alexandria, vol. 2, Warszawa-Wiesbaden, 2005, pp. 28-36. Additiones 7 and 14 contain references to ʾAbäkäräzun of Gabra Gärzän (ca. 1394-1476).
