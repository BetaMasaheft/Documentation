    ff. 4r-18v ዘሄኖክ፡ ነቢይ፡ በረከተ፡ አምላኩ፡ ወኀይለ፡ ረድኤቱ፡ የሀሉ፡ ምስለ፡ ፍቁሩ፡ መዝራዕተ፡ ክርስቶስ፡ ለዓለመ፡ ዓለም፡ አሜን፡ ወአሜን። [Complete]

    Language of text: Gǝʿǝz

    Rubric : The chapter division is only marked in the beginning. It is the same as in the other manuscripts of Enoch.

    The text of this manuscript agrees always with one of the five manuscripts used for Dillmann 1851.

    The title states that the manuscript was produced for Mazrāʿǝta Krǝstos.
