Condition

The Ms. is stained by water. The front board is of slightly irregular shape. The leather cover has been damaged at the spine. The bottom margin of all leaves has been damaged or possibly cut. ff. 13, 96 have been damaged by insects/rodents at the bottom margin. A cut repaired on f. 32.
