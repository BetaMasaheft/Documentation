General description

origin

Original Location: ʾAgamyo Qǝddus Mikāʾel / AGM

Date: 1816-1828

The colophon indicated that the Ms. was written during the time of däğğazmač Säbʾa Gadis (1770-1831) and ʾabunä Qerǝlos (1816-1828).

State of preservation

good

Condition

Many fols. are stained with wax, dirt and water. Traces of a previous overback are preserved on the inner side of the front board. The leather cover is damaged, partly missing (on the spine and boards). The Ms. has been resewn. Fols. 13, 14, 15 are missplaced (e.g., fol. 12vb should have been followed by fol. 16ra). Fols. 12, 66, 67, 104 are mutilated. Fols. 92, 95 are of a smaller size. A cut repaired in fol. 105.

    Description of content

    I) Mäṣḥafä gǝnzät “Book of the Funeral Ritual” (fols. 3ra-116vb) - Discourse of St. Athanasius on death (fols. 3ra-7vb) - Mäṣḥaf zä-wäḍʾat ʾǝm-ʾIyärusalem “Book that Came from Jerusalem” (fols. 12vb-21va) - Homily by St. Jacob of Serug for the funeral of priests and deacons (fols. 54ra-58rb) - Homily by ʾabba Sälama, Metropolitan of Ethiopia (fols. 77rb-79rb) - Antiphons (ʾaryam, mäwaśǝʾt and mǝlṭan) (fols. 112va-116vb) Additiones 1-2; colophon.
