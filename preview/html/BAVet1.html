    Additiones

    In this manuscript there are 1 Invocation, 1 ScribalNoteCompleting, 1 Record, 1 CalendaricNote and 1 Unclear.

    1. (Type: Invocation)

      (ar)

      (en) "Remember, o my Lord, your servant, the poor sinner priest abriyl the Egyptian, delegate of the Alexandrine patriarch and monk of the Monastery of the Virgin Mary of abal al-ayr; and remember, o Lord, those who will commemorate us amongst the Ethiopian fathers".

      The note is written in Arabic on the front board on its inner side. It was drawn up by the Egyptian priest abriyl.

    2. f. 198r

      (Type: ScribalNoteCompleting)

      (gez) (sic) () (sic) (sic)

      The note is written in red ink and states that the Sinodos was completed on the 8th of Gnbot in the year of Adam 6585, year of Matthew. The date can not be assessed with certainty: it might correspond to 1593 AD or be incorrect.

    3. f. 217v

      (Type: Record)

      (gez)

      Historical note on the monastic community of Santo Stefano de' Mori.

    4. f. 219r

      (Type: CalendaricNote)

      (gez) (sic) (sic) (sic) (sic) (sic) (sic) (sic)

      The note refers to the year 6616, year of John. The date can not be assessed with certainty; it might correspond to 1624 AD or be incorrect.

    5. f. 219v

      (Type: Unclear)

      (gez) ... (?) (sic) ()()

      The note is hardly legible because of the faded ink. It mentions Rome and Jerusalem.
