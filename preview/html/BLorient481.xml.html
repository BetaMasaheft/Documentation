    Additiones

    In this manuscript there are 3 Letter, 26 LandGrant, 3 Record, 2 Inventory, 1 ScribalSupplication, 2 Poem and 1 OwnershipNote.

    This volume contains copies of many gweltat, i. e. deeds of gift, grants, or donations of various kings.
