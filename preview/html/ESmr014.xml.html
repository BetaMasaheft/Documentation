50rb, l.15 – 54ra, l.8 Mälkǝʾa Yoḥannǝs Mäṭmǝq “Image of John the Baptist”

Rubrication: Nomina sacra; six lines in both columns of the incipit page (fol. 3r), divided into three groups, alterning with black lines; four lines divided into two groups, alterning with black lines, in both columns of fols. 25r and 30r (beginning of Text II); three groups of two/three lines on fol. 42r (beginning of Text III); the beginning two lines of each miracle within Text III; the first two lines of Text IV; the horizontal dashes above and below Ethiopic numerals; elements of the punctuation signs; the word sälam ; name of one donor (fol. 54ra, ls. 1-2) and of the scribe (fol. 54ra, ls. 5-6).

Incipit: Fol. 50rb, l.15: በስመ፡ እግዚአብሔር፡ እሳት፡ በሐቅለ፡ ሕሊና፡ ነደዱ፨ ወበስመ፡ ማር[fol. 50va]ያም፡ ድንግል፡ መጥበቢተ፡ ዓለም፡ አባዲ፡ ማኅቶተ፡ ፀዳል፡ ዮሐንስ፡ ጽልመተ፡ አባሳ፡ ሰዳዲ፨...Fol. 50rb, l.15: In the name of the Lord, the fire burned in the field of the understanding and in the name of the virgin Mary, John, teacher of the ignorant world, lamp of light, persecuting the darkness of sin...

Explicit: Fol. 53vb, l.16: ...እምእደ፡ መበለት፡ አሐቲ፡ ጸራይቀ፡ ፪ኤ። [fol. 54ra]ወለአመትከ፡ ወለተ፡ ኪዳን፡ አድህና፡ እመከራ፡ ሥጋ፡ ወነፍስ፡ ሊተኒ፡ ለኃጥዕ፡ ገብርከ፡ ጸሐፊሁ፡ አቡነ፡ ገብረ፡ ክርስቶስ፡ ተፈጸመ፡ በሰላመ፡ እግዚአብሔር፡ አብ፡ አሜን: ፨ ፨ ፨Fol. 53vb, l.16: ... from the hand of one nun two thin cakes. And to your maid Wälättä Kidan, save her from the temptation of the body and the spirit; for your sinner servant, its scribe, ʾabunä Gäbrä Krǝstos, it was completed in the peace of the Lord Father. Amen.

Incipit: Fol. 42ra: በስመ፡ʼʼ… ንቀድም፡ በረድኤተ፡ እግዚአብሔር፡ ጽሒፈ፡ ተአምሪሁ፡ ኮከበ፡ ዮርዶኖስ[sic]፡ ገዳም፡ ወማኅቶተ፡ ፄኖን፡ ቅሩበ፡ ሳፈም፡ ብርሃነ፡ ሰፉህ፡ ዓለም፨ [n.l.]እምሰብአ፡ ኅርመት፡ ኅሩም፨ ዘሰደደ፡ ለሥልጣነ፡ [n.l.]ፍትወት፡ ግረም፨Fol. 42ra: In the name of the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit, one God. We start, with the help of the Lord, the writing of his Miracles: the star of the monastery Yordanos, and lamp of Ḍenon, close to …, the light of the wide world. From the man of the illicit ceremony, which he banished, to the power of the terrible concupiscence. …

Explicit: Fol. 50rb, l.3: ...ወአውደቆ፡ በጊዜሃ፡ ውስተ፡ ዓቢይ፡ ጸድፍ፡ ወሞተ፡ እኩየ፡ ሞተ፨ ወተአውቀት፡ ዛቲ፡ ተአምር፡ ውስተ፡ ኵሉ፡ በሐውርት፡ ጸሎቱ፡ ወበረከቱ፡ የሐሉ፡ ዓመቱ፡ ወለተ፡ ጻድቃን፡ ለዓለመ፡ ዓለም፡ አሜን፨ ፨ ፨ ፨Fol. 50rb, l.3: ... and in that moment he dropped him in a big precipice and he died, he died badly. And this miracle was revealed in all the regions. May his prayer and his blessing be [with] his maid Wälättä Ṣadqan. Forever and ever. Amen.
