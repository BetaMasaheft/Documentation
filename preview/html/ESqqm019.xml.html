Item ms_i1

5r-24r I. Mälkǝʾa mädḫane ʿaläm “Image of the Saviour of the World” Complete

Rubrication: Nomina sacra; name of the scribe; incipits and headings of the texts; elements of Ethiopic numerals (not always); elements of the punctuation signs (not always).

Incipit: Fol. 5r: ሰላም፡ ለዝክረ፡ ስምከ፡ ዘኢረከቡ፡ ተፍጻሜተ፤ መላእክተ፡ ሰማይ፡ ወምድር፡ እለ፡ ለመዱ፡ ስብሐተ፡ መድኃኔ ዓለም፡ ተወከፍ፡ እንተ፡ አቅረብኩ፡ ንስቲተ፤ ዘሰተይከ፡ በእንቲአየ፡ ከርቤ፡ ወሐሞተ።…Fol. 5r: Peace be upon the remembering of your name, For which they do not find end, The angels of the heaven and the earth who are accostomed to glorifying; (Oh) the Saviour of the World, accept the small (thing) that I have brought for you, You, who drank because of me myrrh and gall...

Explicit: Fol. 24: ስብሐት፡ ለከ፡ አምላኪየ፡ በኍልቁ፡ ኵሎን፡ ዓለማት፤ ስብሐት፡ ለከ፡ አምላኪየ፡ በኍልቈ፡ ዕለታት፡ ወአዝማናት፤ስብሐት፡ ይደሉ፡ ለመስቀልከ፡ ሕይወት፡ እስመ፡ በመስቀልከ፡ ድኅነ፡ ኵሉ፡ ፍጥረት። ኦእግዚኦ ዕቀቦ፡ ለገብርከ…Fol. 24: Glory be unto you, oh my God, (as many times as) the numbers of all times! Glory be unto you, oh my God, (as many times as) the numbers of the days and the ages! Glory is fitting for Your Cross of Life, because through Your Cross the whole creation has been saved. Oh Lord, protect your servant...

Item ms_i2

24v-45v II. Mälkǝʾa Giyorgis “Image of George” Complete

Rubrication: Nomina sacra; name of the scribe; incipits and headings of the texts; elements of Ethiopic numerals (not always); elements of the punctuation signs (not always).

Incipit: Fol. 24v: ሰላም፡ ለዝክረ፡ ስምከ፡ ዘሰሌዳ፡ ሞገስ፡ መጽሐፉ፤ ዘያሜኒ፡ ኵሎ፡ ወዘያስተሴፉ። ጥዑመ፡ ዜና፡ ጊዮርጊስ፡ ለሐሊበ፡ ዕጐልት፡ ሱታፉ።Fol. 24v: Peace be upon the remembering of your name, which is the tablet of grace, the book, which makes everyone believe and hope. Sweet [is] the story of George, the companion of the milk of suffering.

Explicit: Fol. 45r-45v: ሰ[ላም]፡ ለ[ከ]፡ ያፈቅረከ፡ ልብየ፡ ወአኮ፡ አፉየ፤ ኢታስትት፡ አባ፡ ማኅሌትየ። ወተወኪፈከ፡ ሀበኒ፡ አስብየ፡ ለነዳይ፡ ጊዮርጊስ፡ ወርቅየ። ኦአምላከ፡ ጊዮርጊስ፡ ዕቀቦ፡ ለገብርከ…Fol. 45r-45v: Peace be unto you; my heart loves you, not my mouth! Do not neglect, oh father, my canticle; And, having accepted it, give me, the poor, my reward, my gold, oh George, ! Oh God of George, protect your servant...

The text corresponds to the so-called Mälkǝʾa Giyorgis zä-śäleda mogäs (cp. Säbatu kidanat...,**No record for ES484504** = 484-504 ** (mss) , no. 28.; Gädlä Giyorgis..., 191-99); a portion of the Mälkǝʾa Giyorgis ḍǝnsätu is added to the end, fol.s. 44r-45v (cp. Säbatu kidanat..., 512-13; Gädlä Giyorgis..., 202-03).

Item ms_i3

fols. 46r-63v III. Ṣälot bäʾǝntä lǝssanä säbʾ “Prayer Concerning the Men’s Tongue” Complete

Rubrication: Nomina sacra; name of the scribe; incipits and headings of the texts; elements of Ethiopic numerals (not always); elements of the punctuation signs (not always).

Incipit: Fol. 46r: በስመ፡ አብ፡ ወወልድ፡ ወመንፈስ፡ ቅዱስ፡ ፩አምላክ፤ ጸሎት፡ በእንተ፡ ልሳነ፡ ስብእ፤ ዘመድ፡ ወባዕድ፡ ምቀኛ፡ ወተንኰለኛ። ከመ፡ ኢይቅረቡ፡ ኀበ፡ ነፍሱ፡ ወሥጋሁ፡ ለገብርከ…Fol. 46r: In the Name of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, One God. Prayer concerning the tongue of the men, of the relative and the outsider, the envious and the malicious, so that that they might not approach the soul and the body of your servant...

Explicit: Fol. 63v: ኦእግዚእየ፡ ኢየሱስ፡ ክርስቶስ፡ በዝ፡ ስምከ፡ ዕቀበኒ፡ እምልሳነ፡ ዘመድ፡ ወባዕድ፡ ምቀኛ፡ ወተንኮለኛ፡ ለገብርከ፡…Fol. 63v: Oh my Lord Jesus Christ, by means of Your name protect me from the tongue of the relative and the outsider, the envious and the malicious, your servant..

  1. Fol. 1rv

    (gez) Fol. 1r: በስመ፡ አብ፡ ወወልድ፡ ወመንፈስ፡ ቅዱስ፡ ፩ አምላክ፡ ጸሎት፡ በእንተ፡ ሕማመ፡ አይነት፡ ባርያ፡ ወ[ነ]ገርጋረ፡ ወአይነ፡ ጥላ፡ ወጊ፡…

    (en) Fol. 1r: In the Name of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, One God. Prayer concerning the illness of the [evil] eye, [of] barǝya (slave), nägärgar (epilepsy) and [from] the eye of the sorcerer…

    : Protective prayer against evil eye and other diseases (incomplete). Crudely written in pen, in a recent hand.

  2. Fol. 2r

    (gez) Fol. 2r: በስመ፡ አብ፡ ወወልድ፡ ወመንፈስ፡ ቅዱስ፡ ፩ አምላክ፡ ይባረክ፡ እግዚአብሔር፡ አምላከ፡ አስራኤል[sic]፡ ጸሎት፡ በእንተ፡ ሕማመ፡ አይነት፡ ባርያ፡ ወ[ነ]ገርጋረ፡ ወአይነ፡ ጥላ፡ ወጊ፡…

    (en) Fol. 2r: In the Name of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, One God, blessed be God, the Lord of Israel. Prayer concerning the illness of the [evil] eye, [of] barǝya (slave), nägärgar (epilepsy) and [from] the eye of the sorcerer…

    : Protective prayer against evil eye and other diseases. Crudely written in pen, in a recent hand, the same as Additio 1.
