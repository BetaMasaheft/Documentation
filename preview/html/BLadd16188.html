      Description of content

      I) Ṭǝbaba ʾIyāsu walda Sirāk "The Book of Sirach" (fols. 3r-51v) II) ʿƎzrā 1, "The Apocalypse of Ezra" (fols. 52r-83v) III) ʿƎzrā 2, "The Apocryphal Book of Ezra" (fols. 84r-106v) IV) "The Book of Ezra and Nehemiah" (fols. 106v-137r) V) Ṭǝbaba Sābelā "The Wisdom of the Sibyl" (fols. 137v-142r).

      Item ms_i1

      Fols. 3r-51v Sirach, Book of [Complete]

      Item ms_i2

      Fols. 52r-83v Apocalypse of Ezra [Complete] Labelled Ezra 1 in Ethiopic; generally referred to as 4 Ezra.

      Item ms_i3

      Fols. 84v-106v Book ofEzra [Complete] Labelled Ezra 2 in Ethiopic; generally referred to as 3 Ezra or Greek Ezra.

      Item ms_i4

      Fols. 106v-137r Book of Ezra and Nehemiah [Complete] It comprises the canonical books of Ezra (1 Ezra) and Nehemias (2 Ezra).

      Item ms_i5

      Fols. 137b-142a Sibyl, Wisdom of [Complete]
