    Description of content

    Item ms_i1

    Rubrication: Nomina sacra; incipits and headings of the texts; short postscripts to the Gospels of Matthew and Mark; chapter numbers (in the “tituli”), titles and the word mǝʿǝraf; elements of the punctuation signs and Ethiopic numbers. On the incipit page of each Gospel, a few lines are rubricated (in groups of two lines, alternating with black lines).

    Explicit:

    Item ms_i2

    - The “tituli” are numbered only when they precede the Gospel text; within the text itself the chapters are not numbered. The number of the “tituli” for the Gospel of Mark (47) is given erroneously. - The introduction to the Four Gospels is missing. - The canon tables are missing.

    Additiones

    In this manuscript there are

    1. Fol. 111vb, l.2

      (gez) Fol. 111vb, l.2: ወከማሁ፡ ይባርኮ፡ እግዚአብሔር፡ ለገብሩ፡ አቡነ፡ አስተአ፡ ድንግል፡ ወአቡሁ፡ አቡነ፡ አሥራተ

      (en) Fol. 111vb: And likewise may God bless His servant ʾabunä ʾAstäʾa Dǝngǝl and his father ʾabunä ʾAśratä …

      . Blessing for the persons mentioned. Written as continuation and part of the preceding Gospel text.

    2. Fol. 137ra

      (gez)

      (en)

      -rb. Record concerning a reconciliation agreement and punishment for its transgressors (?). Written in difficult language, a mixture of Amharic and Gǝʿǝz. The reconciliation took place on Mäskäräm 21, 1889 E.C. (September 30, 1896 A.D.). The note was written down on Ṭǝqǝmt 1 (October 10). The note mentions ras Mängäša (Yoḥannǝs, d. 1906 A.D.), governor of Tǝgray in 1889-1906, as well as a number of geographical locations, including Mǝʾsar Gwǝhila and ʿAddiqäḥarsi.

    Extras

    1. - Fol. 2r. Note: መጽሐፈ፡ ሕንኪ፡ አለሽ፡ በወርሐ፡ መጋቢት፡ ተጽሕፈት፨ - Fols. 78r, 78v, 79r, 79v, etc. Marginal notes repeating chapter titles. - Fols. 92r, 95v, in the upper margin. Corrections in red (?). - Fols. 136v, 137r, in the bottom margin. Pen trials.
