General description

Prayer of the Virgin Mary on Bartos (identified by Ludolf, Dillmann and Zotenberg as Berytus, i.e. Bayrūt, but probably from the Παρθος, cp. Guidi 1888, page 9), by which she delivered the **No record for Person PRS11023Mathias** = Apostle Mathias **. It is used for magical purposes.
