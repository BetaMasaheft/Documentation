General description

The Wǝddāse Māryām is the short title of a famous office of the Ethiopian Orthodox Church, consisting of blessings and praise of the Mother of God and intercessions to her.

In some manuscripts, the lection for Sunday is the first, in others the lection for Monday, but this difference does not constitute two textual traditions.
