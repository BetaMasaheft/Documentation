miraculous water spring [miracle]

Edited by Massimo Villa

General description

A frequent miracle worked by a number of saints (Marha Krestos, Yohanni Mesraqawi, others) is the following: the saint makes miraculous water spring from a rock, and the blessed water has prodigious powers. It heals the ill in various ways, makes an infertile woman pregnant, or even resuscitates the dead.
