    Additiones

    In this manuscript there are 3 ProtectivePrayer, 1 Poem, 1 CalendaricNote, 2 Invocation, 3 OwnershipNote, 4 Correction, 2 Unclear and 1 Supplication.

    1. f. 1r

      The text is poorly legible; most probably written in the same poor hand (h3) as Additiones 2-5, 7, 8. (Type: ProtectivePrayer)

    2. f. 1v

      Hymn honouring Jesus Christ (nǝguś Fema). The text is poorly legible; most probably written in the same poor hand as additio 1 (h3). (Type: Poem)

    3. f. 131r

      Protective prayer against wild animals. The note is written in a mixture of Gǝʿǝz and Amharic; most probably written in the same poor hand as additio 1 (h3). The writer makes various carnivorous animals swear that they will not damage the property of individuals. (Type: ProtectivePrayer)

    4. f. 155r

      Calendaric notes of unclear content. The note is most probably written in the same poor hand as additio 1 (h3). The end part of the notes resembles a magical text (chain of apparently meaningless rhyming words). The last words are in Amharic. (Type: CalendaricNote)

    5. f. 155v

      Divination text (?) The note is most probably written in the same poor hand as additio 1 (h3). (Type: Invocation)

    6. f. 172rb

      Divination text. The note is written in Amharic, in a hand not much posterior to the main hand (h4). (Type: Invocation)

    7. f. 172va

      The ownership note (?) is most probably written in the same poor hand as additio 1 (h3). Owner's name deleted; pledge is added, to denounce the Satan before the church of Māryām Ṣǝyon (in Aksum?). (Type: OwnershipNote)

    8. ff. 172va-172vb

      Protective prayer agains Bāryā and Legewon (Ṣalot ba-ʾǝnta bāryā wa-legewon). The note is most probably written in the same poor hand as additio 1 (h3). (Type: ProtectivePrayer)

    10. Correction indicated by lines above and below the word: ff. 13r, 31r, 122v, 143v, 159vb, 163ra, 166rb. (Type: Correction)

    11. The midpoint of the Psalms (ms_i1.1) (in Ps. 77) is marked on f. 67v.

    12. Omitted letters, words, lines or passages written interlineally, in the main hand (h1): ff. 4r, 6r, 9r, 9v, 11r, 26v, 36r, 45r, 55v, 60v, 61v, 73r, 83v, 84v, 85v, 103r, 105v, 119r, 126r, 139v, 141r, 159va, 160va, 164ra. (Type: Correction)

    13. Omitted letters, words, lines or passages written interlineally, in the secondary hand (h2): ff. 152r, 170va, 170vb, 171ra. (Type: Correction)

    15. f. 155r

      Beginning of an (unfinished) ownership note. Only the initial letters of the owner’s name are readable. (Type: OwnershipNote)
