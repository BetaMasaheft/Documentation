Provenance:

The ms. was written in Aksum and presented by **No record for Person PRS10803Yeshaq** = Yǝsḥaq ** to the Ethiopian community in Jerusalem. From there it passed to Rome together with other mss. and, having been transferred from the hostel of San Stefano dei Mori to the Museo Borgiano, it was stolen and brought to London.

In 1817 the bookseller **No record for Person PRS10804Smith** = J. Smith ** in Drury Lane sold it to Church Missionary Society, which transferred it to the British and Foreign Bible Society, for use in a printed Ethiopic edition.
