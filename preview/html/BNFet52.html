    Item ms_i1.2

    ff. 16r-19r Martyrdom of Peter [Incomplete]

    Language of text: Gǝʿǝz

    Incipit :

    Explicit : ጸሎቱ፡ ወበረከቱ፡ ትዕቀበነ፡ አሜን፡

    This copy ends in the middle of the narration (Peter's speech from the cross, etc. are missing), certainly due to a lacuna in this manuscript's Vorlage. Nevertheless, the usual explicit is placed after the sentence ወገብሩ፡ በከመ፡ ፈቃዱ፡ (ፈቀደ፡?).

    Item ms_i1.3

    ff. 19v-22r Martyrdom of Paul [Incomplete]

    Language of text: Gǝʿǝz

    Explicit :

    The first half of the narration is omitted. The narration starts in the middle of Paul's speech before Nero at fol. 19vb, line 2 (the beginning of the chapter belongs to Martyrdom of Peter).
