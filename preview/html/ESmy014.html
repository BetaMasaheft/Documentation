    Description of content

    Däbrä Maʿṣo Qǝddus Yoḥannǝs Ethio-SPaRe

    MY-014, collection



    3r-32r Mäṣḥafä täklil “The Book of the Marriage Ritual”

    Rubrication: Nomina sacra; name of the reigning monarch; incipits of the texts; directives to the ministers; elements of the punctuation signs; elements of Ethiopic numerals. The text on fol. 3r is written alternately in red and black lines.

    Incipit: Fol. 3ra, l.1:በስመ፡ አብ፡ ወወልድ፡ ወመንፈስ፡ ቅዱስ፡ ፩አምላክ፡ ንቀድም፡ በረድኤተ፡ እግዚአብሔር፡ ጸሒፈ፡ መጽሐፈ፡ ተክሊል፡ ዘውእቱ፡ ሕገ፡ ሰብሳብ፡ ዝንቱ፡ ውእቱ፡ ቀዳማዊ፡ ጸሎት፡ ዘይትነበብ፡ ላዕለ፡ አልባሰ፡ መርዓዊ፡ ወመርዓት፨[Fol. 3ra, l.1]: In the name of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit, One God. Let us commence, with God’s help, to write the Book of the Crowning which is the ritual of marriage. This is the first prayer which is read over the garments of the bridegroom and bride.

    Explicit: Fol. 32ra, l.13: ይበል፡ ካህን፡ ዘቀደሰ። ድኅረ፡ ጸሎተ፡ መባእ፡ ክርስቶስ፡ አምላክነ፡ ዘሖርከ። ውስተ፡ ከብካብ፡ እስከ፡ ተፍጻሜቱ።።።[Fol. 32ra, l.13]: Let the celebrant priest recite after the prayer of the offering: “Christ our God who went to the wedding…” up to the end.
