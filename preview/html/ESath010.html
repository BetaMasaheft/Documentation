    Translation en:

    Written in the main hand in Amharic and in a very poor orthography. The colophon states that ʾAsgädom Ṣaʿǝdu bought the Ms. for 30 Bǝrr from the scribe Qes Ḫaylä Gebrä Maryam (name abbreviated as Q. Ḫ. G. M.). It was than donated to the church of ʾAraʿro Täklä Haymanot. The names of his family members are Gäbrä Rufaʾel, Wäldä Ḥawaryat, Wäldä Gäbrǝʾel, Gäbrä Kidan (of?) Ḥarägä Wäyni, Wälättä Qirqos, Wälättä Mikaʾel.
