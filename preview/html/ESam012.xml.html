General description

origin

Current Location: Asir Mätira / AM

Date: 1671-1692

Metropolitan of Ethiopia (ṗaṗasǝnä) ʾabba Sinoda (in tenure 1671/72-1692) is mentioned on fol. 70rb. The Patriarch of Alexandria (liqä ṗaṗasǝnä) is wrongly (?) identified as ʾabba Sinoda, fol. 70rb.

Condition

The Ms. has been rebound and resewn with a thread of vegetal origin. The boards have been re-utilized; the back board is cracked and has a set of previous sewing attachment holes. Some fols. satined with wax. The bottom edge of fols. 1-2 is damaged by rodents. A hole has been carefully amended on fol. 49.

    (en)

    Fol. 45vb-46r: Ownership note. The note is written in a very crude hand, partly in red. It states that the Ms. was purchased for the money of the church of Maryam ʾAšär Mät(ǝ)ra Däbrä ʾAläma.

It probably refers to the content of the ms. from which the board originates. - Omitted letters or words written interlineally, occasionally over erasures: fols. 5va, 10rb, 18ra, 20ra, 21ra, 21rb, 22vb, 25va, 31va, 41ra, 42ra, 42vb, 50r, 59rb, 62rb. - Erasures: 22vb, 24r, 37va. - Corrections over erasures: 40ra, 47ra. - Cues for the rubricator: fols. 10v, 11r, 12r, 14r, 27r, 28r. - Names erased from fols. 11vb, 12ra. - An extensive note erased from fol. 71r. - Minor notes, writing exercises (in Ethiopic syllabary), doodles: fols. 1rv, 2r, 34r, 43r, 45vb, 68v.
