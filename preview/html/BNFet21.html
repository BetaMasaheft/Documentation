    Additiones

    In this manuscript there are 1 Gloss and 1 OwnershipNote.

    1. At the bottom of fol. 8bis v.

      Note indicating the owner, **No record for Person PRS10812Niqolawos** = Niqolāwos **, whose name has been scratched from the miniature. (Type: Gloss)

      (gez) ... ብሂል ፡ መዋኤ ፡ ሕዝብ

    2. f. 129

      Note written in another hand stating that the manuscript belonged later to a monk, whose name isn't mentioned. (Type: OwnershipNote)

    Decoration

    In this manuscript there are in total 2 notes about miniature.

    Miniature notes

    1. miniature: Miniature showing the manuscript's owner, **No record for Person PRS10812Niqolawos** = Niqolāwos **, prostrating and with a gloriole. His name has been scratched off, but traces of the letters remain. ff. 8bis
