    Other decorations

    1. engraving: Four images engraved on paper of the apostles and the Saviour and eight images engraved on paper of other apostles and Our Lady Mary are glued at the beginning and at the end of the book, respectively. They are of the 16th century.
    2. engraving: Engravings of Our Lady of Sorrows and of the Holy Trinity engraved by **No record for Person PRS11204Graffico** = Camillo Graffico ** and crudely drawn, before f. 1 .
