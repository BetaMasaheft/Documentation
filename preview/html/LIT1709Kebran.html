Titles

  • ክብረ፡ ነገሥት፡gez (Glory/Nobility of the Kingsen, Kǝbra nagaśtgez)
  • شرح سبب انتقال مملكة سليمان ملك اسرائيل الى البلاد النجاشيه التي هي الجبشةar (Šarḥ sabab intiqāl mamlakat Sulaymān malik ʾIsrāʾīl ʾilā bilād an-naǧāšīya allatī hiya al-ḥabašaar)

General description

The Kǝbra nagaśt is a kind of historical novel intended to justify the Solomonic dynasty by demonstrating its Israelite descent. Marrassini 2007

Date

Composed (or translated) on the orders of makwannen Yaʿǝbika Ǝgziʾ in 1321.
