History

Foundation

Date of foundation: 1400-1500

Existed aready in the pre-Christian time as the religious site of South Arabian migrants; the church was founded in a later period.

History

Däbr. A new large church is under construction outside of the compound of the old church, both standing on the rock outcrop (below is the river of No record for Place LOC6519Wafadabbo** = Wafadabbo **).
