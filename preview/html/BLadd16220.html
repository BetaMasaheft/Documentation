The note states: "era of the apostles: 7113 year of mercy; era of Byzantium: 6757 year of mercy; era of Rome: 7075 year of mercy; era of the martyrs: 1375 year of mercy; era of Judith: 273 year of mercy; era of the righteous: 7057 year of mercy; era of the elders: 6713 year of mercy. All these computations fall in the 14th year of reign of Śǝltān Sagad". The note contains some inconsistencies. According to the cataloguer, it was written in 1613, during the reign of king ሱስንዮስ፡ Susǝnyos aṣe ስልጣን፡ ሰገድ፡ Sǝlṭan Sagad I ሱስንዮስ፡ Susǝnyos aṣe ስልጣን፡ ሰገድ፡ Sǝlṭan Sagad I Yāʿǝqob Pedro Páez , here called with his regnal name Śǝltān Sagad.

    Additiones of p2

    In this manuscript there are 1 OwnershipNote and 1 Admonition.

    1. f. 9

      The note is written on the upper margin of the leaf and states that the book belongs to ... Krǝstos, presumably **No record for Person PRS10800Bahreya** = Bāḥrǝya Krǝstos **, whose name appears elsewhere in the manuscript. (Type: OwnershipNote)

    2. f. 46

      Ethical precepts concerning the concupiscence and the suffering of the soul (እምነ፡ ፍትወተ፡ ሕማመ፡ ልብ፡), the desertion of wife and sons, and the open and secret sins. (Type: Admonition)
